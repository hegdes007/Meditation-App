What is Meditation or Dhyana?

Meditation or Dhanya is a practice where an individual uses a technique – such as mindfulness or focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity – to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm, and stable state. Meditation is the habitual process of training your mind to focus and redirect your thoughts.


Why should I do it?

You can use it to increase awareness of yourself and your surroundings. Many people think of it as a way to reduce stress and develop concentration.People also use the practice to develop other beneficial habits and feelings, such as a positive mood and outlook, self-discipline, healthy sleep patterns and even increased pain tolerance.
Have a practice of doing meditation atleast 20 minutes a day for best results.
